    AgriGear's Website Data Policy

    I. Introduction

    AgriDito Company adheres to the Republic of the Philippines, Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173). The purpose of publishing this Website Privacy Policy is to reassure users about the measures taken by AgriDito Company to safeguard their privacy and ensure the security of any data accessed through this website, AgriGear: Farm Tools and Equipment. Rest assured with confidence that we will do everything in our power to protect your privacy and maintain the privacy of any information you disclose with us.

    The AgriGear: Farm Tools and Equipment website is managed by various departments within AgriDito Company, each responsible for specific data collection relevant to their operations. The Office of Information Systems oversees the website's functionality, while the Supply and Inventory Information Systems Management ensures seamless day-to-day transactions. This concerted effort guarantees effective handling of your data and seamless website functionality.

    II. Coverage

    1. All users of AgriGear: Shop for Farm Tools, including those who have registered and actively participate in our community on the Internet, are subject to these rules.
    2. Office of Information Systems,Supply and Inventory Information Systems Management, Human Resources Division, Marketing and Sales Division and Supplier and Inventory Division.

    III. Purpose for Processing Information

    On AgriGear: Shop for Farm Tools, a website devoted to agricultural tools, information processing is necessary to enable smooth transactions and offer users individualized services. This entails order management, payment processing, and effective customer delivery of items. Information processing is also essential for improving user experience, maximizing website speed, and guaranteeing legal and regulatory compliance in the agriculture sector.

    1. Employee Data Collection
    2. Customer Authentication
    3. Customer Data used for Marketing Analytics
    4. Order and Delivery Service
    5. Market of the Products

    IV. Personal Information Collection and Processed

    Personal Information, collects, creates, records, receives, or uses data in a variety of formats, including written documents, images, videos, audio files, and other electronic formats. Company’s Data on Supply and Inventory collects, audits, manages and records data in many formats and any forms to ensure the accuracy and forecast demands of the company’s market.These differ based on the kind of data processed, the reason it is collected, and the amount of time needed to accomplish it.

    Data Collection

    1. Register and Authentication/Log Data for Users (Customers)

      When using the services such as add to cart of a product, checkout, order payment and delivery service by a user, the company collects information including: Name ( Last Name, First Name), Phone Number, Email, Age, Password (encrypted) as the based personal information for register and logging in. This data is collected by email, and electronic website forms provided by the company. This is to digitally store data for future references and authentication, and secure use of the system. The use of this data is to avoid the potential privacy risk such as forgery, tampering of the service and breach to security.

    2. Generated Data Analytics for Marketing from Registered Users(Customers)

      This data is collected as a anonymized data, where there is a bunch of collected data, and the company needed to extract the data for statistical use and comparison like the number of users use the website than the actual shop, the number of users buy this product over the other one, and the number of user wants their products delivered, is to help the company to boost and forecast the market and sales for the website shop

    3. Order and Delivery Personal Data

      When a Registered User (Customer) uses the website, they are able to experience the services such as add to cart and shop. When the customer likes to forgo to use the order and delivery service, the company collects personal information including: Name (Last Name, First Name), Phone Number, Gender, Card Numbers (encrypted), Payment Method, Age, Address and Password (encrypted). This information is to be used to ensure that the transaction information is real and to avoid the risk of forgery, fraud and scamming. The data is collected for digital and anonymized analytics and future reference, and for order and delivery, to have smooth operation/ transaction between service and customers

    Data Use

    1. The use of data that AgriGear Company collects from the forms and services that the registered users and company’s employees use is to have a safe and enjoyable online experience, we want to make sure that everyone is aware of and abides by the rules and the law.

      This Data Privacy Policy of AgriGear Company and the Data Privacy Act of 20212 (RA 10173) of the Philippines regulate the processing, handling, and administration of data that is collected through our website.

    Data Security

    1. The website does not require any personal information from the users in order to access and use it, but it is limited to view the shop display and send messages for support and feedback, email is needed to send the message. There are some pages that can be only accessed by the certain division of the company and registers users using log-in credentials and giving checkout and delivery information. The company is giving the rights of the personal information adhere from the DPA and controls such as, Terms and Conditions, Consent Forms, and Informed Data System for the safety of the users.

      This Data Privacy Policy of AgriGear Company and the Data Privacy Act of 20212 (RA 10173) of the Philippines regulate the processing, handling, and administration of data that is collected through our website.

    V. Storage Information

    Personal Information and Company’s Data on Supply and Inventory is stored on various databases and services. Specifically, the database service: Firebase that adheres with other services that stores information into the cloud. As an e-commerce website with the use of digitized forms and services through databases through Office of Information Systems, some of the personal data are also stored in paper forms like the Human Resource Division, collection of Employee’s data and performance and the Supply and Inventory Information Systems Management, saved paper and manual form of the list of goods to be processed by the Marketing and Sales Division and Supplier and Inventory Division to be digitized and market it online. The website also uses services like email for the confirmation of the transactions and smooth communication.

    All divisions of the AgriGear which collects, stores and handles personal information and company’s data on supply and inventory are required to implement physical, technical, digital security measures to guarantee that personal data is stored securely and that only authorized individuals use it for appropriate reasons
